About Us

For over four decades, Shree Puja Sansar has been the trusted name in authentic Nepali puja ornaments. Our journey began in the ancient alleys of Patan, where our founder, master craftsman Shri Krishna Shrestha, learned the sacred art of brass work from his ancestors.


Today, we continue this legacy by partnering with skilled artisans across Nepal, ensuring each piece — from intricately carved kalash to gleaming brass diyas — is crafted with devotion and authenticity. Our ornaments grace thousands of homes and temples, carrying forward the spiritual heritage of Nepal.
